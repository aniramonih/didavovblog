Jeri Ryan’s Career Highlights and Earnings

Jeri Ryan is one of the successful actresses in TV, thanks to her roles in iconic series like Star Trek: Voyager and Boston Public. Her portrayal of Seven of Nine in Star Trek: Voyager not only boosted the show’s ratings but also solidified her place in the sci-fi genre.

FAQs about Jeri Ryan

What is Jeri Ryan best known for?

Jeri Ryan is best known for her role as Seven of Nine on Star Trek: Voyager and Star Trek: Picard. Her portrayal of the former Borg drone made her a fan favorite and earned her critical acclaim.

What other TV shows has she appeared in?

Besides Star Trek, she has appeared in numerous TV shows, including Boston Public, Shark, Leverage, and Warehouse 13. She has also guest-starred in Law & Order: Special Victims Unit and Helix.

Has Jeri Ryan won any awards for her acting?

Yes, she has won several awards, including a Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress for her role as Seven of Nine in Star Trek: Voyager and another Saturn Award for her role in Star Trek: Picard.

What is her background?

She was born in Munich, Germany, and grew up in a military family, living in various locations across the United States. She participated in the Miss America 1990 pageant, finishing as third runner-up before launching her acting career.
