The Journey Begins

Myq Kaplan, born Michael Kaplan in the serene town of Livingston, New Jersey, embarked on a journey that would see him transform from a linguistics scholar to a wealthy stand-up comedian.

Kaplan’s academic pursuits at Brandeis University and Boston University laid a robust foundation, not just in linguistics, but in the nuanced art of comedy.

Rise to Fame

After winning Boston University’s funniest student in 2005, Kaplan’s career trajectory took a sharp turn towards success.

His engagements at comedy clubs across North America, coupled with his appearances on platforms like Comedy Central’s Live at Gotham, ABC News Now, and notably, The Tonight Show, solidified his presence in the comedy world.

Kaplan’s participation in competitions like the Last Comic Standing, where he placed fifth, and his recognition as Local Comedian of the Year by Boston’s The Phoenix, highlighted his growing influence in comedy.

A Digital and Global Presence
