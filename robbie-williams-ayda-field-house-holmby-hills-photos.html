Robbie Williams and Ayda Field's new $49.5m LA mansion is stunning - photos

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  490 words  |  Tandra Barner

The former Take That singer recently sold several of his properties

    Robbie Williams and Ayda Field recently sparked speculation that they were planning a return to London after selling their homes in Wiltshire and Beverly Hills, but it appears they are staying in Los Angeles for the time-being.

    According to , the couple have splashed out $49.5million (£38.9million) on a Holmby Hills estate that once belonged to actress Fanny Brice, complete with a main house and three separate guest houses to offer more than enough space for them and their four young children.

    Amenities include an outdoor pool and pool house with fitness centre, a tennis court, a chef's kitchen, library and formal dining room that is ideal for entertaining. Elegantly decorated throughout and located in the exclusive neighbourhood that is also home to Kylie Jenner, it's easy to see why Robbie and Ayda snapped up the lavish estate.     The property has a panelled foyer with a spiral staircase and inlaid wood floor. It leads through to the living room and formal dining room, and provides an impressive entrance to the house.

    The living room is spacious and flooded with sunlight courtesy of large windows on either side. With a fireplace at the centre, hardwood flooring and artisan touches throughout, it's clear to see why Robbie and Ayda will have been captivated by the space.

    The library features wood-panelled walls with built-in shelving, cosy reading nooks and window seats, and vaulted ceilings.

    The chef's kitchen features sleek white and grey décor, with an island unit featuring pans hanging overhead at the centre, and pendant lights from the ceiling.

    Robbie and Ayda's new formal dining room features seating for 12, and features a striking scenic wallpaper of trees over water, with a dazzling chandelier hanging above the table.

    There are eight bathrooms in the main property, including a master suite with its own sitting area and fireplace, and separate his and hers bathrooms, including a salon hair station in one suite.

    Another bedroom has access to a private balcony, and also benefits from a log-burning fireplace and its own seating area.

    The garden has an outdoor swimming pool lined by sun loungers and a dining table, with a pool house at one end.

    Inside, the pool house doubles up as a fitness centre with a mirrored wall with a barre, along with armchairs for relaxation. It will no doubt prove popular with Robbie and Ayda, who had a similar gym in their former LA home which they recently sold to Drake.

    The beautiful garden has expansive lawns and a terrace with a seating area, as well as a tennis court, a koi point, and a vegetable and flower garden where the family can grow their own produce.
