Are Johnny Manziel and Nate Fitch still friends? Here is their friendship timeline

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  249 words  |  Tandra Barner

Johnny Manziel and Nate Fitch’s friendship is a story of loyalty, growth, and eventual separation. Their close bond began during their middle school years in Kerrville, Texas, where they discovered common interests such as video games, football, and socializing. As Johnny rose to fame as a quarterback, Nate transitioned from being a friend to an unofficial agent or manager, handling various aspects of Johnny’s public image.

Nate played a crucial role in Johnny’s journey, helping him navigate the challenges and controversies that came with his success. They even found a way to profit from signing autographs despite NCAA regulations, thanks to Nate’s strategic thinking and defense of their actions.

However, as Johnny’s career progressed and he turned pro, their paths began to diverge. Johnny received advice to distance himself from his old management setup and transition to more professional representation. This decision marked a turning point in their relationship, and they gradually grew apart. Despite the separation, there is no animosity between them. They both acknowledge the change and the mutual respect they still hold for each other.

Nate’s words about wishing Johnny the best and knowing Johnny feels the same reflect the enduring bond they share, even if they are no longer in constant communication. Their journey together, marked by loyalty, success, and personal growth, continues to intrigue fans and serves as a testament to the complexities of friendship in the face of evolving circumstances.
