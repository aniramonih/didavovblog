Kimberley Garner Biography

Kimberley Garner is an English Bathing costume designer, Television personality, actress and socialite best known for her appearances on Made In Chelsea and The Write Stuff series. She was born in Kensington in the United Kingdom. Kimberley launched her own swimwear collections in May 2013 and has since made progress by expanding her business by opening more collections. She is also the director of Kimberley London Ltd and Young London Events Ltd. Kimberley is a Christian believer and regular worshipper at Holy Trinity Brompton.
