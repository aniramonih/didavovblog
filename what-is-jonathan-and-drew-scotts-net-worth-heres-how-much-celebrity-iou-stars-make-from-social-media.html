LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: HGTV's famous brothers Jonathan and Drew Scott are real estate moguls as well as famed reality stars. With them being one of the most famed brothers of 'Celebrity IOU' fans wonder how much do their make money from the show. To clear your doubts, the Scott brothers do not make the same amount as each other.

It's often considered by the fans that since both are twins and are in the same business as well as the shows they must be paid the same amount. But, according to our finding, it is not true. Since 'Property Brothers' stars are often seen posting sponsored posts on social media, fans wonder how much they would be earning from each Instagram post.

How much do Jonathan and Drew Scott earn from each post?

Once a person appears on reality TV, he/she starts bagging the fan following on social media in no time. From being a renowned person to being a TV celebrity is not a big way to cover for all those who have appeared on famous shows. Many real estate agents developed a huge fan following ever since they appeared on reality shows. The same happened with the Scott brothers. According to The Things' report, the real estate agents turned reality stars make a whopping amount by posting sponsored posts on social media. Jonathan is reported to make somewhere around $8,871 each time he promotes the business on Instagram. On the other hand, Drew potentially makes somewhere around $8,289 with every sponsored post. 

Why does Jonathan Scott make more money than Drew Scott?

We all know how the algorithm of social media works. The more followers, the more people will get to see your social media posts and stories. And, the more the people see it the more will be the reaction to it, indeed leading to more engagement. And, that's what social media influencers are paid for. Therefore, the one who has more followers on social media platform are paid slightly higher than the one who has less. If looked at the social media accounts of the Scott brothers, Jonathan bags more followers than Drew. Jonathan has 2.7 million followers while Drew has 2.5 million. This gap might look small to you, but it's certainly not that small to get paid a bit higher than the other one. 

What is the net worth of the Scott brothers?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Jonathan and Drew's net worth is calculated to be $200 million. The brothers have appeared in various shows like 'Property Brothers', 'Brother Vs Brother', 'Property Brothers At Home', 'Brothers Take On New Orleans', and now will be seen in 'Celebrity IOU'. Besides the real estate business, they also have a home furnishing line, Scott Living. They build and sell luxury homes. "At least once a week I have a major athlete or CEO from a Fortune 500 company reaching out and saying that they want us to design a home," Jonathan said to Forbes. Nothing is off-limits in the Scott brothers' business, whether it be a 20-car garage, a million-dollar theater, or even a helipad.
