Is Ellen Ochoa Still Alive?

Ellen Ochoa first breathed on May 10, 1958. She is a Hispanic engineer, former astronaut and former director of the Johnson Space Center. Ellen was the 1st Hispanic director and the 2nd female director of Johnson Space Center.
