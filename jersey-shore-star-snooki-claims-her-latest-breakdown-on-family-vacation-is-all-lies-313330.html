Jersey Shore Star Snooki Claims Her Latest Breakdown On Family Vacation Is All Lies

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  421 words  |  Martina Birk

The cast and crew of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation is currently filming Season 3, and TMZ recently got their hands on a video from a neighbor that features Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi losing it on Angelina Pivarnick. After the video started making its way around the internet, Snooki decided to post her reaction and explanation on her YouTube channel, and she says that the video doesn’t tell the whole story.

“I can’t tell you what it’s about, but you know, she’s coming at me and in my head I’m thinking — mind you, I had like two bottles of wine — but in my head I’m thinking, ‘I didn’t leave my kids to deal with this high school drama,” said Polizzi. “I got up and I walked out and had a fit.”

The reality star explained that she had anxiety about leaving her two-month old baby Angelo to hang out with her friends and film scenes for Season 3, and when Pivarnick started “giving [her] crap” it was a bad combination.

Polizzi added that she went home to take care of her baby that night – as well as her 6-year-old Lorenzo and 4-year-old Giovanna – but producers will make it look like she slept there when the show airs.

In the clip, Snooki is screaming while leaving the Jersey Shore house, telling everyone that she is “done” with the show. As soon as she started watching the clip, she admitted that she is “embarrassed” by her behavior, and the video made her “literally want to die.”

Polizzi also called out the TMZ reporters, wondering why they were so serious about the video, and asking “who cares?” when they discussed if the fight between her and Pivarnick was because of something new that happened or if it was old issues between them.

Snooki says that TMZ is always rude to her, so she never talks when they are around. She says that she was pleasantly surprised that they didn’t say anything horrible about her while reporting this latest story.
