Swizz Beatz and Alicia Keys have five children between them, namely Prince Nasir Dean, Kasseem Dean Jr., Nicole Dean, Egypt Dean, and Genesis Dean.

The hip-hop power couple has two kids together, namely Egypt and Genesis, while the oldest three, Prince Nasir, Kasseem, and Nicole, are from Swizz Beatz’s previous marriage and romantic relationships. Swizz Beatz and Alicia Keys have worked together to build a unified family unit for all the children that they have, and that has positively impacted them all.

Swizz Beatz and Alicia Kids Got Married in 2010

Hip-hop power couple Alicia Keys and Swizz Beat might not be as famous as Beyoncé and Jay-Z or as loud, but there is definitely no doubt about the huge influence they both wield in the culture. Having met all the way back in the eighties as ambitious teenagers, both of them have risen to take up huge spots in the echelons of Black American music and worldwide as well.

A fifteen-time Grammy Award winner, Alicia Augello Cook was born in Harlem, New York, on 25 January 1981. Famous for chart-topping singles like ‘No One’, ‘Girl on Fire’, ‘Falling’, ‘My Boo’, and ‘Empire State of Mind’, Alicia has been running the music game for a long time since the release of her first album, Songs in a Mirror in 1991. Nearly forty years and countless awards later, she is happily married to the legendary music producer Swizz Beatz. Swizz is famous for producing the beats for DMX’s trademark spitfire rap bars. He has worked with all the big shots in the industry, including Jay-Z, Lil Wayne, Whitney Houston, Beyoncé, Eve, and many others.

Swizz and Alicia began seeing each other in 2008, shortly after the former got divorced from his first wife Mashonda. In 2010, they surprised everyone by announcing their engagement. In that same announcement, the couple revealed that they were also expecting their first child together. News like this is usually welcome; however, if there are only mere months between divorce and moving on, it isn’t taken too well by the public. All the blogs were afire with the juicy scandal that gleefully painted Alicia as a husband snatcher, and it certainly didn’t help that things were already awry between her and Mashonda. Not that it deterred the lovers in any way, though.

On the last day of July 2010, Alicia and Swizz were hitched in holy matrimony at a private wedding ceremony held near the Mediterranean Sea. Fast forward to twelve years later, the ripples appear to have settled. Swizz and Alicia are still as strong as ever, and they have since added one more child to their cute family.

How Many Children Do Swizz Beatz and Alicia Kids Have Together?

Alicia Keys and her husband are certainly blessed with good looks and talent. They appeared to have passed these blessings onto their two sons, named Egypt and Genesis. Egypt Daoud Dean is the couple’s first son, who was born three months after his parents’ wedding in 2010. Genesis Ali Dean followed four years later, in 2014.

Meet Swizz Beatz and Alicia Keys’s Children

The talented pair are raising quite a brood between them. It’s no new knowledge that Alicia is the proud mother of two boys, but her husband definitely takes the cake when it comes to the number of children they have. Swizz has five kids from 4 different women, staying true to his Muslim belief, which permits polygamy. Apart from his two sons with the famous singer Alicia, he also has other children from his past relationships; two sons and one daughter. This brings the total number of kids in the musical duo to 5.

He was not even yet nine years old then, but it was clear to all that the boy was definitely going places.

His mother has also proudly said that her first child was very good with musical instruments, especially the piano. It’s every parent’s joy to see their children follow their path with as much joy and passion as Egypt has, and we really hope he keeps it up.
