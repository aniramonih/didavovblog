Aishwarya Arjun is an Indian actress who mostly appeared in Kannada and Tamil films. she is best known as the daughter of popular film actor Arjun Sarja. She made her first movie in the Tamil film Pattathu Yaanai in 2013.

Aishwarya Arjun Age

Aishwarya was born on 10th February 1990 in Bangalore, Karnataka. She is 30 years old as of 2022 and her zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Aishwarya Arjun Education Background

Aishwarya completed her higher education at Sacred Heart Matric. Hr. Sec. School, Church Park in Chennai. After that, she furthered her education at Stella Maris College in Chennai to finish a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.) She also holds a degree in Fashion Management/Technology from Birmingham City University, Birmingham, England.

Aishwarya Arjun Family

Aishwarya Arjun was born into a family of talent in acting, no wonder she grew up becoming a sensational actress. Aishwarya’s family has made a lot of impact in the Indian movie industry. Her father is the popular actor and director Arjun Sarja while her mother’s name is Niveditha Arjun who also worked as a dancer and actress in several Indian films. Aishwarya has a younger sister named Anjana. Popular actor Dhruva Sarja and the late Chiranjeevi Sarja are her cousins.
