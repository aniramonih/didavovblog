Kelly McGillis is an American actress who is perhaps best known for her portrayal of Rachel Lapp in Witness (1985), Charlie in Top Gun (1986), and Kathryn Murphy in The Accused (1988). Besides that, she has appeared in more than 2 dozen shows and films including Private Sessions (1985), Made in Heaven (1987), The Monkey’s Mask (2000), and Z Nation (2014).
