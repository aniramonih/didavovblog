The Golden Bachelor Winner for 2023

The winner of the inaugural season of The Golden Bachelor is Gerry Turner, a 71-year-old resident of Indiana. The unique dating show, which featured contestants aged 65 and above, concluded with Gerry choosing Theresa Nist, a 70-year-old Financial Services Professional from New Jersey, as the winner.

The couple got engaged, and reports indicate that they are still together. The runner-up of the show was Leslie Fhima from Minnesota, while Faith Martin from Washington was eliminated earlier in the competition. Despite speculations among fans on social media, Reality Steve confirmed Gerry’s choice and engagement with Theresa.

Who is Theresa Nist?

Theresa Nist, a 70-year-old Financial Services Professional from Shrewsbury, New Jersey, was a finalist in The Golden Bachelor Season 1. She vied for Gerry’s heart in the final round of the show.

Theresa, known for her kind-hearted nature, places great importance on her family, particularly her children and grandchildren, finding joy in their success. Describing herself as optimistic and supportive, she is open to embracing love once more after experiencing loss.

In this new phase of her life, she hopes to find a partner who will not only be a romantic companion but also her best friend. Theresa cherishes life’s simple pleasures and is eager to share them with a special someone.

When not with her family, Theresa enjoys activities such as playing board games, gardening, reading romance novels, and even hula hooping. She is seeking a genuine connection and is optimistic about sparks flying when she meets the Golden Bachelor.
