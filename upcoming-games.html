 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  906 words  |  Martina Birk

What are the big games coming in 2024?

We're now a little ways into 2024, and what a year it's shaping up to be! Now, we look ahead to March, which sees the long-awaited release of Dragon's Dogma 2.

We're now well into the new year, with the next few months shaping up to be a great line-up for gamers.

From Dragons Dogma 2 to GTA 6, there's plenty of games to look forward to whether you play on console or PC. Though some are much closer to release than others.

Below, we’ve collated release dates and windows for the biggest confirmed games of 2024 and beyond. So whether you’re playing on PC or consoles, there will likely be at least one game on this list that you’ll want to mark on your calendar. Here's the upcoming games to look forward to in 2024, and beyond!

  • Dragon's Dogma 2 - March 21, 2024 (PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC)
  • Princess Peach: Showtime - March 22, 2024 (Switch)

  • Alone in the Dark - March 20 (PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC

  • Rise of the Ronin - March 22, 2024 (PS5)

  • South Park: Snow Day - March 26 (PS5, Xbox Series X|S, Switch, PC)
  • Acolyte of the Altar - March 25, 2024 (PC)

April

We don't have many games pinned for April yet, but that'll likely change in the coming weeks. Here's what we know about so far:
