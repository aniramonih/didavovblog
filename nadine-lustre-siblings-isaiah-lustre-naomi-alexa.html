Nadine Lustre’s Personal Life Unveiled: A Glimpse into Her Close-Knit Family, Including Her Siblings and Their Heartbreaking Loss.

Nadine Lustre is a multi-talented artist from the Philippines who has made a name for herself in the music and acting industry. 

Born in Quezon City on October 31, 1993, She is known for her captivating performances in movies and TV shows, including Never Not Love You, On the Wings of Love, and Indak.

The 29-year-old’s exceptional talent has earned her numerous awards throughout her career. She has received 3 FAMAS Awards, 1 German Moreno Youth Achievement Award, and a Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Award.

With her ability to create chart-topping hits, the singer has been affiliated with major music labels such as Viva and Careless Music Manila and has released dozens of popular songs.

Her most notable works include Paligoy-ligoy, No Erase, and Hanap Hanap.

Aside from her musical prowess, Nadine’s stunning beauty has garnered her significant attention. She has been recognized as the “Most Beautiful Woman in the Philippines” on multiple occasions by the public.

Nadine Lustre Siblings: Meet Her Family

Nadine Lustre was raised in Quezon City with her younger sister, Naomi Alexa Lustre, and two younger brothers, Ezequiel Lustre and Isaiah Lustre.

Her mother is Myraquel Paguia-Lustre, and her Father is Ulysses Lustre, a mechanical engineer.

Not much is known about Nadine’s family; she prefers to keep them away from the media and rarely publicly talks about them.

Nadine attended Diliman Preparatory School during childhood and later pursued Communications Arts Major in Films at Colegio de San Lorenzo on Congressional Avenue.

The singer’s journey into the entertainment industry began at a young age. When she was nine years old, she debuted as a host at RPN network’s children-oriented show, Storyland. 

Lustre occasionally shares pictures of her loved ones on social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook. Her Instagram profile, @nadine, has 11 million followers. 

The pop star is not yet married, but she is currently dating French businessman Christopher Bariou.

Nadine Lustre Brother Committed Suicide

Despite her success and fame, Nadine Lustre has also gone through a tragic incident in her personal life.

On October 7, 2017, her 16-year-old brother, Isaiah Paguia Lustre, allegedly committed suicide. He reportedly shot himself and passed away.

Nadine shared a cryptic message on her Facebook page after the heartbreaking incident that read, “#KeepGoing.”

According to online reports, Nadine’s younger siblings, Ezekiel and Naomi, played tablet games when they heard gunshots from Isaiah’s room.

They rushed him to Pacific Global Medical Center, but he was declared dead on arrival.

The Actress’s family revealed that Isaiah had been irritable, silent, and often locked himself in his room in the weeks leading up to his death.

This suggests that the cause of the suicide may have been mental health problems.

In 2019, Nadine opened up about her late brother, saying that the loss destroyed her.

“He was going through a lot based on what he was writing down. But he never opened up to us. I think the most important thing you can do with someone who has depression is to talk to them.”

The singer also stated that she had gone through a bout of depression three to four years ago due to work pressure and other issues.

The loss of her brother has undoubtedly been a difficult time for Nadine and her family. Their story serves as a reminder of the importance of mental health awareness and the need to speak out about one’s struggles.
