Biography of Rose Zhang

Rose Zhang is a young professional golfer from the United States who has quickly risen to prominence in the golfing world. She was born on May 24, 2003, in Arcadia, California, and started playing golf at a very young age.

In 2020, despite the challenges presented by the COVID-19 pandemic, Rose continued to make waves in the golfing world. She won the prestigious Women’s Southern Amateur Championship and earned a spot in the U.S. Women’s Open, where she finished tied for 11th place.
