Sadorra was interviewed by a Dutch news show for a documentary about the hundreds of homeless people living in the network of tunnels.

“I actually got very famous. Maybe a little too famous,” Sadorra said.

It’s unknown how long Sadorra has lived in the tunnels or how she got there in the first place. The actress is originally from Clarksville, Tennessee.

However, despite the meager conditions including no access to running water, Sadorra is happy with where she lives. She said there is a tight-knit community of people who were more accepting. 

“It’s not as difficult as you might think, everybody’s really respectful,” said Sadorra. “Everybody’s good to each other, which I don’t think you find much [above ground].”

Sadorra explained that “hardships build camaraderie”, believing that living underground in the tunnels has allowed her to make genuine friends.
