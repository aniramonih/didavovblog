Joseph James Rogan aka “Joe Rogan” (born 11 August 1967) is an American Stand-up Comedian, Actor, Writer, Podcaster, TV Host, Sports commentator, and businessman from Bell Canyon, California. He has appeared in various comic roles in Hollywood movies and TV shows like Hardball (1994 TV series), Here Comes the Boom (2012), NewsRadio television sitcom and Zookeeper (2011).

The 51-year-old Stand-up Comedian has also won awards like MMA Personality of the Year (2011, 2012, 2014 & 2015) & Best Television Announcer (2010 & 2011).

Joe Rogan Profile, Age, Parents & Early Life

Joseph James Rogan was born on the 11th of August 1967 in Newark, New Jersey, USA. Therefore, Joe Rogan’s age is around Fifty-One (51) years old, as of 2018. His grandfather has moved his family to Newark in 1940. He belongs to Multiracial Mixed Italian and Irish Ethnicity (descent) group.

Comedian Joe is the only son of his parents. His dad has worked as a Police officer in Newark. His father “Joseph Rogan” has left her mother when he was very young. At the age of 5 years old, his parents have settled their divorce.

Therefore, her mom has raised him as a single parent. In addition, his dad has never contacted him since he was seven years old.

As per his education, he has attended Newton South High School to complete his high school graduation. In his school days, he has learned Martial Arts, Karate and Kickboxing. He also represented his institute at various taekwondo competitions. At the age of 19 years old, he has won US Open Championship tournament in the lightweight category.

Later in college days, he has dropped out before completing his bachelors’ degree graduation from the University of Massachusetts Boston.
