Kate Bolduan is an American journalist and news anchor. She is well known for her appearance on At This Hour with Kate. Working for CNN previously, she also co-presented New Day and The Situation Room. Scroll down to know in detail about the former congressional correspondent.

Early life and Education

Kate Bolduan was born as Katherine Jean Bolduan on 28th July 1983, in Goshen, Indiana, the United States of America, where another journalist Natasha Bertrand was born. Born to her American parents Nadine Bolduan and Jefferey Bolduan, she is of White ethnicity and holds American nationality. 

Her father, Jeffrey Bolduan is a doctor and mother Nadine serves as a nurse. Kate grew up with her three sisters Jana Lomax, Courtney Bolduan Carpenter, but the name of the youngest in not disclosed yet. 

Kate studied at Goshen High School based in her hometown. Later she attended George Washington University and received her bachelor's degree in journalism in 2005. 

During her days at the University, Bolduan actively played Volleyball and joined the women's volleyball team. She also spent some time in Spain and learned the Spanish language.

Kate kicked off her career as a general assignment reporter for WTVD-TV in Raleigh, North Carolina. Later, she started working on television channels as a production assistant for MSNBC and NBC News. Also, explore, Blayne Alexander is a news correspondent for NBC News.

Additionally, Kate is also famous for her appearance at Dateline NBC, House & Garden Magazine, and NBC Nightly News.
