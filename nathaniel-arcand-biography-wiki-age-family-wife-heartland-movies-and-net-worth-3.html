Who is Nathaniel Arcand? Nathaniel Arcand Biography and Wiki

Nathaniel Arcand is a Canadian actor who is well known for his first major role in the Canadian drama series North of 60, in which for three seasons he played William MacNeil, smart-mouthed and cocky, a troubled, misunderstood teen. He was nominated in 1997, for a Gemini Award in the category “Best Performance in Supporting Role of a Dramatic Series” for the North of 60 episodes “Traces and Tracks.”
