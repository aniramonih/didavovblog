Who Is Marie Osmond? Get to Know the Talented Singer and Actress

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  917 words  |  Billy Koelling

Is there anything Marie Osmond can't do? She's a country singer, an actress, a doll designer, an entrepreneur, an author, a Nutrisystem spokeswoman, and a loving mom! Since the former child star definitely isn't slowing down anytime soon, scroll down to see your biggest questions about Marie, answered.

Is Marie Osmond married?

Marie actually has the sweetest love story with her husband, Steve Craig. The pair got married in 1982, but unfortunately, they divorced in 1985. Marie then married Brian Blosil in 1986, and they spent 21 years together before splitting in 2007. In 2011, Marie remarried Steve — while wearing the same dress from their first wedding! The pair still seems head over heels for each other, too. Marie recently posted a photo of herself hugging her hubby with the caption, "on vacation with my sweetheart." So cute!

Who are Marie Osmond's children?

Marie has quite a brood! She shares one son with Steve named Stephen, who was born during their first three-year marriage. Marie had two biological kids with Brian named Rachael and Matthew, and they also adopted five children: Jessica, Michael (who sadly took his own life at just 18 years old), Brandon, Brianna, and Abigail. Today, all of Marie's kids are relatively private people living normal lives. The singer also has three grandchildren.

Did Marie Osmond win Dancing With the Stars?

Marie competed on the fifth season of Dancing With the Stars in 2007, but unfortunately she didn't win. She definitely held her own, though! She came in third place, and made a huge splash when she actually passed out after one performance! Luckily she was okay, she simply said she "forgot to breathe."

When is Marie Osmond's birthday?

Marie just recently rang in her 58th birthday on Oct. 13, 2017. She celebrated by going on vacation with her husband and thanking friends for their kind wishes.

Who are Marie Osmond's parents and siblings?

Marie's parents were Olive May and George Virl Osmond. The Utah couple raised their nine kids in the The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The whole family was involved in showbiz, except for her brothers Virl and Tom, who were born deaf. Alan, Wayne, Merrill, Jay, Donny, and Jimmy Osmond were all performers, but Donny was most famous for being a pop heartthrob and singing duets with Marie. Sadly, Olive died in 2004 and George passed away in 2007, just days before he was supposed to join his entire family (including his 55 grandkids and 48 great-grandkids) on the Oprah Winfrey Show.

What is Marie Osmond's real name?

Believe it or not, Marie Osmond is actually a stage name. Her real name is Olive Marie Osmond and she was named after her mother. As the only girl in the family, it makes sense that Marie was given her mother's name. However, she's used Marie since the very start of her career.
