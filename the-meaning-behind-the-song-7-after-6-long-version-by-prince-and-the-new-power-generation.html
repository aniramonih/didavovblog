The Meaning Behind The Song: 7 (After 6 Long Version) by Prince and the New Power Generation

When it comes to iconic artists who have left an indelible mark on the music industry, Prince is undoubtedly among the greats. Known for his boundary-pushing and genre-blending style, Prince consistently pushed the envelope and created music that spoke to the soul. One such song that showcases his brilliance is “7 (After 6 Long Version)” by Prince and the New Power Generation.

The lyrics of “7 (After 6 Long Version)” are poetic and evocative, painting a vivid picture in the listener’s mind. The song begins with the chorus, where Prince declares, “All seven and we’ll watch them fall, they stand in the way of love and we will smoke them all.” This imagery sets the tone for the song, expressing defiance against obstacles that hinder love and embracing the power to overcome them.

In the first verse, Prince describes an angel descending upon him, holding the key to compassion and peace. He also mentions an army and animosity that stand against love, but remains steadfast in the belief that they will ultimately fall. This juxtaposition of angelic intervention and opposition creates a sense of hope and resilience.

The chorus then repeats, emphasizing the unity between Prince and his love interest, asserting that no one in the universe can compare to their bond. They vow to love each other through all space and time, assuring that one day, all obstacles will be conquered.

In the second verse, the lyrics take a darker turn as Prince sings about a plague and a river of blood, symbolizing evil and suffering. Despite this, he reassures that with every breath, the voice of many colors will sing a bold song, inspiring the listener to persevere amidst adversity.

Throughout the song, Prince’s use of vivid imagery and symbolism adds depth to the lyrics. The number “seven” is used repeatedly, representing completeness and spiritual fulfillment. It also refers to the seven deadly sins, suggesting the obstacles that love must overcome.

Personally, “7 (After 6 Long Version)” holds a special place in my heart. The song’s powerful lyrics and Prince’s captivating vocals have the ability to transport me to another world every time I listen to it. The message of triumphing over adversity and embracing love resonates deeply with me, reminding me of the strength we all possess to overcome life’s challenges.

Furthermore, Prince’s musical genius shines through in this song as he effortlessly blends different genres, infusing elements of funk, rock, and pop. The blend of his signature falsetto vocals, intricate guitar solos, and infectious rhythms create a mesmerizing musical experience.

Released as a single in 1992, “7 (After 6 Long Version)” showcases Prince’s artistic brilliance at its best. The song stands as a testament to his ability to create music that not only sounds captivating but also carries a profound message.

In conclusion, “7 (After 6 Long Version)” by Prince and the New Power Generation encapsulates the essence of Prince’s artistry: pushing boundaries, defying conventions, and delivering messages of love and triumph. The song’s powerful lyrics, combined with Prince’s musical genius, create an enduring piece of art that continues to resonate with listeners to this day.
