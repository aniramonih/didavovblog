Tamra Judge Plays Never Have I Ever: Watch the Video

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  339 words  |  Martina Birk

Welcome to the jungle, we’ve got fun and games! The Real Housewives of Orange County star Tamra Judge revealed to Us Weekly during a game of Never Have I Ever that she once made a sex tape in the jungle. She also revealed that she used to work at a fast-food restaurant and that she loves to do Silento’s “Watch Me (Whip/Nae Nae)” dance.

“I just want to change the rules a little bit,” Judge prefaced before starting the game. “I’m just thinking that we drink for every question. I love breaking rules!”

Speaking of breaking rules: The Bravolebrity, 48, told Us that she once got so high that she had to run out of the supermarket for fear of getting kicked out.

“My cousin got me so stoned and we went to the grocery store and I knocked over one of those big Ragu jars,” she admitted. “I was laughing so hard because I was stoned. We ran! I was like, ‘Get me out of here!’”

But Judge has her share of regrets. Reminiscing about the RHOC episode where she threw wine in Jeana Keough’s face, she said, “It was not one of my prouder moments.”

“I was so sure that I was gonna get arrested,” she said of the incident. “So I went home. I didn’t take my false eyelashes off [or] my makeup [and I] laid my hair out. If I’m gonna get arrested, I’m gonna look damn good doing it!”

The Housewife also revealed that she worked at Burger King for two years, where she ate the chicken sandwich every day for lunch, and that she knows how to Nae Nae. “It’s the best song ever,” she laughed.
