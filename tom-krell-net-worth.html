Tom Krell Full Overview and Wiki

Early Beginnings

Born in Boulder, Colorado, Krell started his musical career from a philosophical and artistic angle, which is evident in his work’s introspective nature. In 2009, under the moniker How to Dress Well, he began releasing music via his blog, which quickly caught the attention of both fans and critics.

His early works, characterized by their lo-fi production and emotional vulnerability, set the stage for his later successes.

Rise to Critical Acclaim

His debut album, Love Remains, released in 2010, was a compilation of his early EPs. It garnered critical acclaim, receiving a Best New Music nod from Pitchfork Media and praise from various other outlets for its innovative blend of sounds and haunting vocals.

This album established Krell as a serious artist capable of blending soundscapes and ambient music with poignant lyrics.

Expanding His Musical Horizons

Following the success of his debut, Krell’s subsequent albums, such as Total Loss (2012) and What Is This Heart? (2014), saw him exploring broader sonic territories.

These works combined his signature emotional and experimental pop with more accessible electronic beats, making his music appealing to a wider audience. His 2014 album was particularly notable for its accessible sound, which did not compromise on his experimental roots.
