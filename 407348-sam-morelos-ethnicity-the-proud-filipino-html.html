Sam Morelos' Ethnicity: The Proud Filipino-American Is Excited for Representation in the Arts

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  505 words  |  Kelle Repass

Sam Morelos was born and bred in the U.S.; even so, the actress likes to stay connected to her roots. Sam Morelos's ethnicity is Filipino; her parents moved from the Philippines to the U.S. before she was born.

Sam Morelos fully embraces her Filipino heritage, finding joy in staying connected to her roots. As the daughter of Filipino immigrant parents, the promising actress shares her appreciation for her cultural background.

Growing up, she immersed herself in Filipino TV programs, which played a vital role in language acquisition. Through watching these shows, she learned several phrases and even started referring to her older sibling as "Kuya," the Filipino word for brother.

Sam Morelos' Ethnicity Is Filipino

Morelos and her family actively prioritize staying connected to their cultural heritage. The actress expressed her enjoyment in participating in activities that foster this connection, such as visiting Filipino supermarkets for grocery shopping.

With Morelos' mother's family residing in the Philippines and her father's family in Los Angeles, she said having an extended family nearby has been valuable. Conversing in Tagalog, the native language, with her relatives further strengthens their bond.

The actress is proud to represent her community and culture internationally through Netflix. She revealed that growing up, seeing limited Filipino representation on screen validated her career choices.

In December 2018, Morelos and her mother entertained the audience with an amazing "What About Love" performance on stage.

When Morelos fast saw the animated Filipino baby in "Pixar's Float," she got emotional as it was the first time she saw her features represented. According to the actress, being relatable to others, and sharing experiences and appearance, shows the immense value of representation.

Sam Morelos Wants to Inspire Other Filipino Artists

Morelos aspires to leverage her platform to assist aspiring young artists in pursuing their dreams. She acknowledges lacking professional experience and industry connections when she started but now aims to utilize her voice to support others.

Being able to represent her culture and community on a global scale through Netflix has provided her with opportunities. Morelos finds inspiration in proudly speaking about her Filipino heritage, hoping to encourage fellow Filipino artists to embrace and express their cultural identity and follow their dreams.

Sam Morelos' Mother Supports Her Career

Sam Morelos' mother is the eldest of five children. The actress once revealed in a touching tribute that her mom took the role of her mother figure long before she and her siblings were born.

In her heartfelt post, the actress expressed deep admiration for her mother's ability to inspire strength and courage, especially during difficult times. She gratefully acknowledged her mother's unwavering support over the years and recognized the precious gift of music that her mother bestowed upon her.
