Kowloon Fight Video 2023: Watch Kowloon Riot: Kowloon Restaurant Fight Saugus

On the bustling Thanksgiving Eve, Kowloon Restaurant in Saugus witnessed a chaotic scene as a brawl broke out among patrons. The eatery, known for its family-friendly ambiance, turned into an amateur WWE ring, with at least one man left bloodied amid the frenzy.

A video posted on social media captured snippets of the intense altercation, featuring multiple individuals exchanging punches. The footage depicts a man with a bloodied face caught in a headlock, while two women attempt to intervene. The situation escalates when a glass bottle shatters on top of the two men, adding to the mayhem.

In a separate video, a police officer is seen tackling one of the brawlers in an effort to quell the disturbance. Six Saugus police officers swiftly entered the scene, successfully deescalating the situation and restoring order to the restaurant.

Saugus Police Condemn the Thanksgiving Eve Disturbance:

Saugus Police Chief Michael Ricciardelli expressed his disapproval of the incident in a statement to NBC Boston. “The Kowloon is a family-friendly restaurant; unfortunately, a few unruly patrons ruined a nice evening for many others,” he stated. The chief deemed such a disturbance on Thanksgiving Eve as “unacceptable and disgusting.”

Restaurant Closure and Police Response:

As chaos ensued, Massachusetts state troopers arrived to disperse the crowd after the restaurant’s owners decided to close down the establishment early. Kowloon owner Bob Wong expressed his dismay at the turn of events, emphasizing the restaurant’s popularity and the challenges that come with it. The 1200-seat restaurant, located just 11 miles Northeast of Boston, is renowned as “one of the premier multi-concept dining establishments in the United States.”

Fallout and Investigation:

Police detained and questioned several young men involved in the brawl, but as of now, no arrests have been made. The exact number of people injured remains undisclosed, but a felony investigation has been initiated due to the use of a glass bottle during the altercation.
