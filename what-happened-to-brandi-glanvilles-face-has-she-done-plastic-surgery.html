What Happened to Brandi Glanville’s Face -Facial Burns

It’s no surprise that the former Real House of Beverly Hills actress had a burn mishap last year, and she spoke up and published pictures on the media.

It was revealed to her that she had accidentally touched a psoriasis light, causing second-degree burns all over her face.

She mistook the doctor’s request for the light to be kept on for 17.3 seconds when, in fact, the doctor requested it to be kept on for only seconds.

However, she is in good health and prepared to participate in season two of The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip.

Has Brandi Glanville Done Plastic Surgery And Nose Job?

Brandi Glanville dispelled allegations that she underwent cosmetic surgery by saying that a gum infection is to blame for the swelling on her face.

The “Real Housewives of Beverly Hills” alum tweeted on Monday, stating she had a periodontal (gum) surgery on the right side of the actress’s mouth on the rear right side because she developed a cavity below the gum line.

Moreover, Glanville explained her face was bloated and infected. The model is now on her second round of antibiotics.

Brandi also shared with her fans that she would be the first to announce to her followers when she would get plastic surgery.

After posting video clips from her “Unfiltered” podcast to her Instagram page over the past week, fans started to assume that Glanville had undergone surgery.
