Who is Michael Norell?

Michael Alden Norell (October 4, 1937 – May 12, 2023) was an American screenwriter, actor, and executive producer. He is best known for his role as Captain Henry "Hank" Stanley in the Jack Webb-produced television series Emergency! from 1972 to 1978. Norell was born in Wallace, Idaho, and raised in Arlington, Virginia. He attended Washington and Lee University, where he majored in journalism. After graduating, he enlisted in the United States Army and served for five years.

After leaving the Army, Norell moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting. He landed a role in the television series Police Story, and then was cast as Captain Stanley in Emergency! Norell played Stanley for six seasons and was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series in 1976.
