Vivek Ramaswamy Ethnicity: Where Is He From?

Vivek Ramaswamy hails from a family of Indian origin. The politician was born to Indian-Hindu immigrant parents who migrated to the United States.

His family roots trace back to Tamil-speaking Brahmins originally from Kerala.

His father, V. Ganapathy Ramaswamy, holds a degree from the National Institute of Technology Calicut and had a successful career as an engineer and patent attorney at General Electric.

Vivek’s mother, Geetha Ramaswamy, a Mysore Medical College & Research Institute graduate, pursued a career as a geriatric psychiatrist.

The Ramaswamy family’s journey to the United States began with their immigration from the Palakkad district in Kerala, India.

Vivek Ramaswamy family had an ancestral home in a traditional agraharam located in the town of Vadakkencherry.

This cultural background rooted in Kerala’s rich heritage has undoubtedly influenced Vivek Ramaswamy’s life and values.

Vivek and his younger brother were raised in Cincinnati, Ohio, by their parents, who instilled in them the importance of education and hard work, values deeply ingrained in their Indian heritage.

Meet Vivek Ramaswamy Brother Shankar Ramaswamy

Shankar Ramaswamy is the brother of Vivek Ramaswamy and shares his elder brother’s drive for success and innovation.

Like Vivek, Shankar has achieved remarkable success in business and medicine.

Shankar is the co-founder and CEO of Kriya Therapeutics, a biopharmaceutical company specializing in the development of gene therapies for common diseases such as diabetes and obesity.

His educational journey took him to Harvard University, where Shankar earned a bachelor’s degree in biology.

He later pursued a medical career, obtaining his MD from Brown University.

Shankar’s involvement in the pharmaceutical industry runs in the family.

He was an integral part of the early team at Roivant Sciences, the pharmaceutical company his older brother Vivek founded.

Furthermore, Shankar has showcased his entrepreneurial spirit by co-founding Strive Asset Management, an investment firm.

The bond between Shankar and Vivek extends beyond their family ties.

They share a deep passion for innovation, entrepreneurship, and the pursuit of positively impacting society.

Together, they have contributed significantly to the fields of medicine and biopharmaceuticals, leaving an indelible mark on the healthcare industry.

In conclusion, Vivek Ramaswamy’s family background is steeped in Indian heritage, with roots tracing back to Kerala.

His parents’ hard work and values played a pivotal role in shaping his life.
