Al Harris is an American football coach and former player. He currently works as the Dallas Cowboys’ (NFL) assistant secondary coach. From 1998 to 2011, Harris worked 14 seasons in the NFL as a defensive back.

He was a player for the Philadelphia Eagles, Green Bay Packers, Miami Dolphins, St. Louis Rams, and Tampa Bay Buccaneers. After playing for Green Bay in 2007 and 2008, he was chosen for the Pro Bowl.

In 2007, the AP also selected him as a second-team All-Pro. However, his fans are curious about the girl he’s married to. In this post, you’ll find all the details about it.
