Contributions to Classical Music

Nelly Ben-Or is not just a pianist; she is a pillar in the classical music industry, especially known for her long-standing position at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama.

Her career began in her native Poland but flourished spectacularly in London where she has influenced countless students with her mastery of the piano and her profound musical insights.

Over the years, Nelly has not only performed in prestigious venues worldwide but has also made significant contributions to music education, shaping the next generation of classical musicians.

The Impact of the Alexander Technique on Piano Playing

One of Nelly’s most notable contributions to music is her integration of the Alexander Technique into piano playing.

She was among the first to apply this method specifically to piano teaching, demonstrating its effectiveness through her students’ successes and her own performances.
