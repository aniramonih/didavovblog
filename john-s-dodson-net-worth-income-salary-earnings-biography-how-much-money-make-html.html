Jack Dodson (May 16, 1931 – September 16, 1994) (born John S. Dodson in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania) was an American television actor best known for his portrayal of the mild-mannered Howard Sprague on The Andy Griffith Show and its spin-off Mayberry R.F.D. Jack Dodson was born on May 16, 1931 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He was married to Mary Dodson, who worked as an art director for television, from 1959 until his death in 199Andy Griffith cast Dodson in the Howard Sprague role in 1966 after seeing him perform in the Broadway production Hughie the Musical.  Andy Griffith Show episode “Lost and Found” featured Dodson as insurance agent Ed Jenkins, who was played by Dodson himself. Dodson has also had guest appearances on My Friend Flicka, The Virginian, Maude, Barney Miller, Archie Bunker’s Place, Newhart, Mr. Belvedere, Matlock, Mama’s Family, and St. Elsewhere, among other programmes.
