Lisa Vanderpump Refuses Stassi Schroeder's Money on 'Vanderpump Rules': Watch

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  222 words  |  Martina Birk

You can't go home again? Stassi Schroeder unsuccessfully attempts to make amends with Lisa Vanderpump on Vanderpump Rules' Monday, February 1, episode, as seen in Us Weekly's exclusive sneak peek. 

The clip shows Schroeder, 27, stopping by Sur to atone for having previously insulted Vanderpump, 55, and her restaurant's staff. Schroeder was last seen in the volatile season 3 reunion before making her first appearance on season 4 last week.

"You had a boss and a good friend, and you f–ked it up," Vanderpump tells her. "You really did. When you were 22, you didn't have a bloody pot to piss in, you know? And you came in here, and I really had your back. I wish I hadn't, to be honest."

However, Schroeder doesn't feel the need to be indebted to the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star.

"She gave me a job — as a waitress," Schroeder tells the camera. "What do I owe you again?"

Schroeder then acknowledges that Vanderpump paid off Schroeder's ex to keep him from leaking a sex tape to the public, so she tries to repay her former boss.

"I don't want your money," Vanderpump replies. "It's an insult."
