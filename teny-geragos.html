Mark Geragos' Daughter, Teny Geragos Married Husband Ashwinn Krishnaswamy In July 2021

Teny Geragos is a trial lawyer and is widely known as the daughter of an American lawyer, Mark Geragos, and his loving wife, Paulette Geragos. Her father, Mark who is known for working as a lawyer for more than three decades first came to the highlight after representing former President Bill Clinton's business partner, Susan McDougal in the early 2000s.

For now, Geragos works as a managing director of the law firm Geragos & Geragos, which is located on Figueroa Street in Downtown Los Angeles. Just like him, his daughter Teny has followed in his dad's footsteps. Currently, she is associated with Brafman & Associates, PC, working as an attorney.

Today here is everything you need to know about Teny.

Is Teny Geragos Married? 

Yes, Teny Geragos is a happily married woman. She has tied the wedding knot with her boyfriend turned husband, Ashwinn Krishnaswamy in 2021. He is a co-founder of OKLAHOMA SMOKES. The couple was a high school sweetheart of NYU.

Back on May 27, 2019, they became engaged where Mark posted Teny's picture on his Twitter saying that he cried like a Baby and Won’t get much better than EMac getting married. And Teny was beautiful, he added. 

Finally, Geragos got married to her college sweetheart Ashwinn on Sunday, July 4, 2021, in the presence of their family and friends. According to Reef's Twitter post, the newlywed's duo was seen dancing together. He wrote that his best friend from NYU married his college sweetheart last week. And (surprise!) the pair came out to Dreams for the first dance. 

How Much is Teny Geragos Net Worth?

Working as an attorney at New York-based criminal security firm Brafman & Associates, P.C., she must be making a decent amount of money. Her net worth is listed at $100 thousand. She does works from state criminal cases to complicated federal matters and federal appeals.

She earned her California State Bar license in Sep. 2017 and worked with Brooklyn Defender Services. She also worked as a Judicial Extern for the Central District of California at the US District Court. She also applied for a job as an assistant at the US Attorney's Office but was not hired.

On the other hand, her father, Mark Geragos has a whopping net worth of $25 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He has earned his worth throughout his successful career as a criminal defense attorney. He is a managing partner in the law firm Geragos & Geragos since 2007. 

His many notable clients are Scott Peterson, Jeremy Mayfield, Michael Jackson, Roger Clinton Jr., Jussie Smollett, Scottie Pippen, and Jorge Ramirez. Apart from that, he has made apperances on TV shows such as Anderson Cooper 36, Larry King Live, 60 Minutes and also hosts the Making the Case on CNN.

Teny Geragos: Bio, Wiki and Early Life

Teny Geragos' exact date of birth is still unknown to the media. She is the single daughter of Mark Geragos, and his wife, Paulette Geragos, who is the owner of jewellery line Bling by the Yard. 

Teny was raised in New York, NY alongside her brother, Jake Geragos. In 2016, she graduated from the Loyola Law School with a Doctor of Law – JD. While studying there, she worked at the Supreme Court Justice John G. Ingram as a Judicial Extern. 

Also, she earned her degree from the New York University in Art History and Media, Culture & Communications.
