He attended Erasmus Hall High School before transferring to Abraham Lincoln High School. Neil was offered a fencing scholarship at New York University. There he was a pre-med major.

Diamond was in his senior year when Sunbeam Music Publishing offered him a job. He accepted it and dropped out of college.
