Rebecca Herbst: Professional Career

The beautiful Herbst decided to be an actress from the early age of six. At that time, she appeared in more than 60 advertisements. Moreover, she has appeared in TV shows as guests such as Highway to Heaven, L.A. Law, Beverly Hills, Step By Step; Boys Meets World, Sister, Sister, Days of Our Lives, and Brotherly Love.

Not to mention, Herbst has also starred in films including Kaleidoscope, Why Me? And Hefner: Unauthorized. Also, the blue-eyed actress was featured in the 1995 music video ‘Naked’ of Goo Goo Dolls.

Moreover, Herbst was a regular cast in the second season of the Nickelodeon show Space Cases. The show aired from 1996-97, where she portrayed the role of Suzee. On August 1, 1997, she began as a series regular as Elizabeth Webber on the ABC Daytime soap opera General Hospital. 

Likewise, Herbst won the Soap Opera Digest Award for Outstanding Younger Lead Actress in 1999. Not to mention, she was nominated in the many awards, including the Daytime Emmy Award in 2007 and 2012.
