The pair welcomed William in June 1982 and expanded their family two years later with the arrival of Harry in September 1984. However, the duo’s world shifted when their mother tragically died in 1997 when they were teenagers. Despite going through their respective hardships, the siblings overcame their grief and blossomed into mature young men. As they grew up, both Harry and William weren’t shy about showing some skin.

The Duke of Sussex for his part, has been known to show off his chest more often. As the siblings grew older, their desires to begin the next phase of their adult lives emerged. William was the first to settle down with his wife, Princess Kate, whom he wed in April 2011. The couple went on to welcome three children: Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis, who were born in July 2013, May 2015 and April 2018, respectively.

For Harry, finding The One took longer. After various high-profile relationships, he began a whirlwind romance with Meghan Markle in 2016. Shortly after they began dating, the pair took a romantic getaway to Jamaica to enjoy the sandy white beaches where the former British military officer went shirtless while enjoying the bright blue waves. After two years together, the former military pilot and the Suits alum tied the knot in May 2018. They welcomed their first child, son Archie, in May 2019. Their family expanded in June 2021 when the California native gave birth to daughter Lilibet.

While Harry and Meghan became closer, the Spare author’s relationship with members of the royal family grew tense. A family feud sparked after the couple decided to step back from their senior royal duties in 2020. The Archewell cofounders made the decision permanent in 2021 and moved to California to raise their family.

Matters got worse for Harry as he opened up about his strained relationship with his brother, father and more of the royal family in his tell-all memoir, Spare, which was released in January 2023. Harry recounted numerous arguments between himself and his brother over the years from their childhood to his dramatic exit.
