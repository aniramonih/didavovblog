Ekta Kapoor (also spelled as Ektaa Kapoor) is an Indian film producer, TV producer, director, writer, singer, and actress, and fashion designer, best known as the managing director of the company Balaji Telefilms, created by her dad, Jeetendra, who was a prolific Indian actor. At 1st, Ekta fell in love with being a TV show and soap opera producer, such as Mano Ya Na Mano (1995), Kanyadaan (1999), Koshish… Ek Aasha (2000-2002), Kesar (2002-2004), Kkusum (2001-2005), and Kavyanjali (2005-2006). Ekta was also a series producer for K. Street Pali Hill (2004-2006), Kahiin To Hoga (2003-2007), Kyunki… Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi… (2000-2008), Kasamh Se (2006-2008), Bandini (2009-2011), and Pavitra Rishta (2009-2014).

In the early 2010s, she was also a series producer for Kalash-Ek Vishwaas (2015-2017) and Yeh Hai Mohabbatein starting in 2013, as well as Kumkum Bhagya starting in 2014. Under the banner of Balaji Motion Pictures, she was also a film producer for more than 35 movies between 2001 and 2019. Ekta also started a video-on-demand platform and application named ALT Balaji, through which she has also created/directed/produced numerous web series between 2017 and 2019. Ekta also created her own fashion line under the name EK Label. She has also amassed a large social media fanbase with more than 3 million followers on Instagram and more than 600k followers on Facebook.

Some of those were including Love S*x Aur Dhokha (2010), Once Upon a Time in Mumbaai (2010), and Shor in the City (2011). This led her to be reluctant to produce the movie Half Girlfriend (2017), which ended up being profitable.
