Who is Cierra Ramirez?

Cierra Ramirez is an American actress and singer. She plays the role of Mariana Adams-Foster in the Freeform television series The Fosters. Ramirez also has an outstanding music career and has released several songs.

Likewise, she also has opened the act for bands Earth, Wind and Fire, Chicago, and American Idol’s Ruben Studdard. The actress is famous also for appearing in movies like ‘All In’  and the drama film ‘Girl in Progress’.
