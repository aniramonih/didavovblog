Gary Oldman is an English actor and filmmaker who is best known for portraying roles such as Sid Vicious in Sid and Nancy, Rosencrantz in Rosencrantz & Guildenstern Are Dead, Joe Orton in Prick Up Your Ears, Lee Harvey Oswald in JFK, Count Dracula in Bram Stoker’s Dracula, James Gordon in The Dark Knight Trilogy, Sirius Black in the Harry Potter series, Ludwig van Beethoven in Immortal Beloved, and George Smiley in Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy. He has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films suchn as Prick Up Your Ears, The Scarlet Letter, Interstate 60, Batman Begins, Dead Fish, The Book of Eli, A Christmas Carol, Red Riding Hood, Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy, The Space Between Us, The Hitman’s Bodyguard, The Woman in the Window, Friends, and many more. For his acting skills, Gary has been honored with numerous awards such as the Academy Award, British Academy Film Award, Golden Globe Award, and Screen Actors Guild Award.

Education

Gary Oldman has attended West Greenwich School in Deptford. He left school at the age of 16, owing to financial constraints and instead, worked in a sports shop.

Later, he won a scholarship to attend the Rose Bruford College in Sidcup, South East London. He graduated with a BA in Acting in 1979.

    Gary Oldman Facts

  • Gary Oldman has been known for his acting skills but he had also written and directed ‘Nil by Mouth’ (1997) based particularly on his own story. The film gave Kathy Burke, the Best Actress prize at the Cannes Film Festival 1997.
  • Oldman has problems with alcohol and was once arrested for drunk driving in Los Angeles. He was released on bail on August 9, 1991, the next day of the arrest. His co-passenger was Kiefer Sutherland.
  • Oldman tried to get rid of alcoholism during 90s and got himself checked into Marworth treatment facility in Waverly Township, Pennsylvania in 1993.
  • Oldman was a member of the jury at the Cannes Film Festival in 1993.
  • He was ranked #72 by Empire magazine in the 100 Sexiest Stars in Film History list in 2007.
  • Oldman applied for higher studies at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts (RADA). He was denied admission and was asked to find a new career other than acting.
  • During his early years, Oldman took up various jobs to meet financial needs. He worked on assembly lines, as a porter in operating theatres, sold shoes and worked as a butcher.
  • Gary has appeared in David Bowie’s music video ‘David Bowie: The Next Day’ (2013) along with Marion Cotillard.
  • Gary Oldman is an alumnus of Royal Shakespeare Company and Royal Court Theatre.
  • Gary Oldman has also produced a live performance by Jack White, former member of ‘White Stripes’ in collaboration with Vevo and YouTube.
  • He has done voiceover work in video games for characters such as Sgt. Jack Barnes in Medal of Honor: Allied Assault – Spearhead, Ignitus in The Legend of Spyro: A New Beginning, and Sgt. Viktor Reznov in Call of Duty: World at War.
