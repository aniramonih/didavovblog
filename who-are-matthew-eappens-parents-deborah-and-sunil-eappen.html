Who Are Zosia Mamet Parents David And Lindsay?

Zosia’s mother, Lindsay Crouse, born on May 12, 1948, in New York City, is a highly skilled actress celebrated for her exceptional performances both on stage and in movies.

Though specifics about her financial success remain undisclosed, her profound impact on the performing arts is evident. Crouse’s dedication to her craft breathes life into characters, contributing to the creative legacy of the Mamet-Crouse family.
