When Did Teyana Taylor & Iman Shumpert Meet?

In 2011, Teyana Taylor and Iman Shumpert met at a New York party. Taylor told VladTV in a 2014 interview, “He came up to me and tried to talk to me, but I was in a relationship.”

On Shumpert’s 24th birthday, Taylor and Shumpert made their official social media debut together. Taylor captioned a picture, “Happy Birthday baby.. #ILoveU.”

A few days later, Taylor appeared on Shumpert’s profile for the first time. The NBA player shared a little video (provided below) of Taylor with a string of smiley faces.

At the 2014 BET Hip Hop Awards at the Boisfeuillet Jones Atlanta Civic Center in Atlanta, the athlete and the singer made their debut appearance in public as a couple. Shumpert posted a black-and-white picture from the evening to Instagram the next day.

When Did Teyana Taylor & Iman Shumpert Get Married?

A few months after their pregnancy announcement (September 2, 2015), Shumpert and Taylor revealed they were engaged during their baby shower at the Museum of Contemporary Art in Cleveland. Taylor posted a picture of her ruby ring on Instagram, featuring a band covered in diamonds and gold.

During a 2016 interview Teyana Taylor and Iman Shumpert revealed they secretly got married in September 2016. After some time Taylor shared a photo from the wedding on Instagram.

How Many Kids Does Iman Shumpert and Teyana Taylor Have?

Iman Shumpert and Teyana Taylor announced on September 2, 2015, that they were expecting their first child together. The NBA player disclosed that the couple’s daughter arrived in January 2016 by sharing an ultrasound picture.

Shumpert wrote, “I couldn’t be happier than to share my first child with the woman I intended to have my family with.”

Two days later, Taylor posted a video of Shumpert singing to her belly on Instagram with the caption, “This is what goes down when I’m with my 2 favorite people in the world… The only thing that calms baby girl down is hearing her father sing Smiley Faces by Gnarls Barkley to her lol.”

On December 16, 2015, Teyana Taylor and Iman Shumpert welcomed their first baby together. Taylor disclosed the news in an Instagram post and wrote, “On Dec 16th at 6:42 a.m. in our bathroom, Junie decided she wanted to take her first breath into this world. She came out as a wonderful surprise to everyone!” 

On June 12, 2020, Teyana Taylor and Iman Shumpert revealed they’re expecting a second child in the “Wake Up Love” music video. At the conclusion of the video, Taylor kisses Shumpert and Junie in bed before revealing her baby bump for the first time by removing her blanket and raising her top.

On Sep. 6, 2020, Shumpert announced on Instagram that the couple welcomed another baby girl together. Iman shared a video of their newborn daughter with the caption, “At 3:28 am on Sept 6th, 2020 Rue Rose decided that the baby shower thrown for her and mommy was too lit..”

Taylor, Shumpert and their two daughters appeared on the April 2022 cover of Ebony. The couple talked about Black love and juggling their passions with marriage and children.

What Happened Between Iman Shumpert and Teyana Taylor?

Taylor shared the news of the couple’s breakup on Instagram in September 2023. She wrote:

“We are still the best of friends, great business partners and are one hell of a team when it comes to co-parenting our 2 beautiful children. Most importantly we are FAMILY & in the 10yrs together, 7yrs married we ain’t ever played with or about THAT.”
