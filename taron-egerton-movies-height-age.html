Best known for his character of Gary “Eggsy” Unwin/Galahad in the Kingsman series, Taron Egerton is a renowned Welsh actor and singer. Since the short-term debut role in 2013, Taron has starred in plenty of movies and TV shows.

Taron Egerton’s name in the acting business came to highlight following his lead role in the 2014-aired British drama, The Smoke. While he kept on shining the progressive career with numerous projects, the actor found it difficult to nourish his relationship life.

Taron Egerton Short Bio: Age, Height, Family

Taron Egerton, age 29, was born as Taron David Egerton on 10 November 1989 in Birkenhead, Merseyside to parents from nearby Liverpool. His father and family ran a bed-and-breakfast while his mother engaged herself in social services. Though from England, Taron considers himself to be Welsh.

He grew up in Wirral Peninsula before moving to Lianfairpwllgwyngyll, on the Welsh island of Anglesey. There, he joined primary school. Later, when he was 12, he moved to Aberystwyth and attended Ysgol Penglais School.

Standing tall at a height of 5 feet and 9 inches (1.75 m), Taron graduated with BA (Hons) Acting from the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in 2012.

Taron Egerton Career, Net Worth

Taron Egerton’s acting career began with his two-episode stint as Liam Jay on British detective drama Lewis in 2013. A year later, he made a grand and to the point, his paramount appearance as Gary “Eggsy” Unwin on spy comedy flick Kingsman: The Secret Service. The film was directed and produced by British filmmaker Matthew Vaughn on a production cost between $81 to 94 million. Significantly, the action spy flick took away the box-office by storm and made $414.4 million.

Taron even got the fortune to play a starring role of Michael Edwards in the 2016 biographical sports comedy-drama film, Eddie the Eagle. The English actor and director, Dexter Fletcher shot the movie on a budget of $23 million collected almost twice as $46.1 million at the box office. In the same following years, he voiced the animated character of Johnny in the 2016 America cartoon film, Sing; In the same movie actor, Nick Kroll has voiced the character of Gunter.

He continued the role in the second edition of the movie named Kingsman: The Golden Circle in 2017. The second installment of the much popular franchise made almost four times a box-office collection of $410.9 million on a budget of $104 million.

He is set to fire up the showbiz with the upcoming biopic of Elton John, Rocketman. While fans can catch his voice as Moomintroll in 2019 Finnish-British animated drama series Moominvalley.

Also known for his 2018 appearance as Robin Hood in the same-named action/adventure, Taron Egerton enjoys a commanding net worth $6 Million.
