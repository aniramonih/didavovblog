News: Bethesda Game Studios eagerly launched Starfield, a highly anticipated video game, through Bethesda Softworks. Marking Bethesda’s inaugural franchise in a quarter-century, the game ventured into outer space, unveiling on September 6, 2023, to acclaim from critics. The expansive open world, celestial setting, and enchanting soundtrack garnered particular praise.

Within the Starfield universe, players are immersed in a myriad of quests and missions, such as the ‘Escape From the Endless Voyage’ and ‘First Contact’ quests. This article serves as a comprehensive guide, offering detailed walkthroughs for both quests to assist players in navigating the challenges and reaping the rewards within the game.

Starfield Escape From the Endless Voyage Walkthrough:

Initiating the ‘Escape From the Endless Voyage’ quest requires players to first locate the ECS Constant after equipping it with a Grav Drive, typically found by checking the orbit over Paradiso. Once aboard the ECS Constant, players must seek out Janet Yang in the Green Room, identifying her as a passenger on the ship.

Engaging with Janet Yang, players should listen to her expressed desire to depart the vessel, officially activating the quest. The subsequent objective involves finding Captain Diana, who may be on the bridge or in her adjoining private quarters. Convincing Captain Diana to permit Janet Yang’s departure becomes the next crucial task.

Players are presented with two options for persuading Captain Diana: the Persuade Option or the Potatoes Option. Utilizing persuasion skills proves to be the simpler and more cost-effective approach, although players also have the alternative choice of offering Captain Diana 50 potatoes. Once Captain Diana is convinced, players should convey the positive outcome to Janet Yang.

As a gesture of gratitude, Janet Yang rewards players with a selection of ancient Earth items upon quest completion. With Janet Yang now free to leave the ECS Constant, players can resume their odyssey in the expansive galaxy of Starfield, delving into more encounters with NPCs and embarking on additional quests.

Commencing the ‘First Contact’ quest initiates with a distress signal from Jiro Sugiyama, Porrima 2’s chief of security. Players are tasked with navigating to the Porrima solar system, situated a few systems to the right of Alpha Centauri. Upon reaching their destination, players must promptly respond to the distress call and descend to the surface of Porrima 2.

On Porrima 2, players delve into an investigation of an unresponsive ship orbiting the planet. Despite attempts to establish communication, the ship remains silent. Boarding the ECS Constant, an enigmatic spacecraft, reveals a human crew that has been in transit for two centuries in pursuit of a new habitable world.

The subsequent imperative is to engage in a conversation with Captain Brackenridge, who seeks players’ assistance in negotiating with Paradiso CEO Oliver Campbell for the approval of the new habitable planet. Returning to Porrima 2, players make their way to the Paradiso Hotel to meet with Oliver Campbell, where the fate of the ECS Constant crew is subject to discussion.

In the quest, players face three distinct resolution options:

Option 1: Acquire a Grav Drive – This is considered the optimal choice. Players should engage with Bennu St. James at HopeTech on planet Polvo in the Valo system. They can purchase a Grav Drive for the ECS Constant at either 40,000 Credits or persuade Bennu to lower the price to 25,000 Credits. After obtaining the Grav Drive, players prepare the ship in the ECS Constant’s Engineering bay and then update Captain Brackenridge with the positive news, receiving Antique Earth items as a reward.

Option 2: Settlement Deal – Indentured Servants – This alternative involves collecting resources such as Fiber, Sealant, Iron, and Lithium to integrate colonists as indentured servants. However, this choice displeases the crew and the ECS Constant, and players must submit the resources and accept the consequences.

Option 3: Overload the Reactor and Blow Up the Ship – In this option, players must either pickpocket or confront Amin Kazemi to obtain the Reactor Computer key. They subsequently initiate Reactor Overdrive in the Engineering room, hack the Captain’s Terminal on the bridge to confirm self-destruction, and escape the ECS Constant before it detonates. The reward for this drastic action includes 6,500 Credits and unrestricted access to the resort from Oliver Campbell.

Upon selecting a resolution, players witness the consequences of their decision, with varied aftermaths based on the chosen option. Players can track the ECS Constant’s location in the galaxy for potential future missions.

Throughout both quests, completing objectives and making choices significantly influences the trajectory of players’ journeys in Starfield. It is crucial to weigh the ramifications of decisions and thoroughly explore the expansive galaxy to fully immerse themselves in the game.

Starfield delivers an action-packed RPG experience with a seamless blend of first-person and third-person perspectives. Featuring over 1,000 planets, moons, and space stations to explore, players encounter diverse NPCs and assemble a unique crew with distinct abilities. The game’s depth is enhanced by customization options, including protagonist customization, skill development, and weapon enhancements. Resource management, outpost establishment, and spaceship customization are essential elements to master in this enthralling galactic adventure.

 Starfield offers an immersive and captivating video game experience set in the vastness of outer space. The ‘Escape From the Endless Voyage’ and ‘First Contact’ quests present thrilling challenges, and players, guided by the walkthrough, can navigate the game’s expansive galaxy, making informed decisions to continue their exploration.

FAQs

Q: How can I commence the ‘Escape From the Endless Voyage’ quest in Starfield?

A: To initiate the quest, locate the ECS Constant after equipping it with a Grav Drive and find Janet Yang in the Green Room on board.

Q: What choices are available to persuade Captain Diana to allow Janet Yang to leave the ship?

A: The available options include using the Persuade Option or presenting Captain Diana with 50 potatoes.

Q: What is the optimal choice for resolving the ‘First Contact’ quest in Starfield?

A: The optimal choice is to purchase a Grav Drive for the ECS Constant and share the positive news with Captain Brackenridge.
