Andy Roddick is an American former world No. 1 tennis player.

Roddick was ranked in the year-end top 10 for nine consecutive years and won five Masters titles in that period.

He retired from professional tennis following the 2012 US Open to focus on his work at the Andy Roddick Foundation.

In retirement, Roddick played for the Austin Aces in World Team Tennis in 2015. He was also the 2015 and 2017 champion of the QQQ Champions Series.

In 2017, Roddick was inducted into the International Tennis Hall of Fame.

Andy Roddick Children: Meet Hank Roddick and Stevie Roddick

Andy Roddick has been married to model and actress Brooklyn Decker since April 2009, and together they share two children; Hank Roddick and Stevie Roddick.

Hank Roddick

Hank Roddick is the son of Andy Roddick and Brooklyn Decker.

Hank Roddick is 6 years old as of 2021.

Stevie Roddick

Stevie Roddick is the daughter of Andy Roddick and Brooklyn Decker.

She was born in November 2017 in United States.

Stevie Roddick is 4 years old as of 2021.
