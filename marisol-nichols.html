Riverdale actress, Marisol Nichols is an American public figure. She is widely recognized for her role as Nadia Yassir on the Fox series 24. Currently, she is playing the role of Hermione Lodge in the American teen drama television series, Riverdale. Some notable works are Big Momma's House 2, Delta Farce, The Road Home, The Sex Monster, Alias, The Twilight Zone, and many more.

Talented actress, Nichols was born on 2nd November 1973 in Rogers Park, Chicago, Illinois, United States. She grew up in Naperville Illinois with her two younger brothers. She was raised by her mother who is of Mexican ancestry and stepfather Randy. Furthermore, she is also a Scientologist, humanitarian, and activist who is the executive director and founder of the Slavery Free World foundation.

Furthermore, some of her co-stars from Riverdale are Hayley Law, KJ Apa, Cole Sprouse, and Sukyong Melton.

Enjoying life as a single mother

Forty-seven years old actress, Nichols is currently enjoying her life as a single mother. She is a proud mother of her child's daughter. Despite having numerous relationships and affairs in the past, she now prefers to live a singular lifestyle. Maybe in the future, we can see her with her loving partner but for now, she is still single. 

As 2021 has started, Marisol is quite busy with various projects and works.  She is appearing as one of the cast in the series, Riverdale, and is heavily involved in the post-production of an upcoming American horror film, Spiral. Moreover, she loves to spend her free time reading books and hanging out with her mates and family. She often updates her fans about her daily life through her official Instagram account.

Ten years of married life ended with the director, Taron Lexton

Nichols is not only famous for her acting career but she equally displayed in the media with her relationships and affairs. In 2005, Nichols met director Taron Lexton for the very first time. Soon with a couple of drinks and dates, they got involved in a very serious attachment. After dating for a couple of years they accepted one another as husband and wife in April 2008. 

The beautiful wedding ceremony was held at the Church of Scientology's Celebrity Centre in Los Angeles. However, after ten years of their beautiful nuptial journey they called it quit to their relationship and parted. In November 2018, Marisol filed for divorce and it was finalized in 2019. Despite their split, the couple is still the parents of one daughter, Rain India whom they welcomed back in September 2008.

Furthermore, Nichols was married to her first husband, Andrea Sorrentino in November 1999. According to her, she met Andrea at the set of My Father's Shoes in Italy. However, their relationship lasted for a couple of years, and later they got divorced.

Versatile actress, Nichols has a net worth of $3 Million as of 2021. She has earned all of her fortunes through her outstanding career as an actress. She is part of various blockbuster movies and TV series and is one of the experienced actresses in the industry. As she plays an important and lead role in the movies and series, she is paid a hefty amount of remuneration. 

Her estimated annual income is over $300,000 as of now. Furthermore, she is currently living in her Los Angeles home. In April 2019 she paid, $1.775 Million for a contemporary-style home in Valley Village, California. Later in July 2020, she listed that home for sale for $1.9 Million.
