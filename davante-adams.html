Who Is Davante Adams?

Davante Adams is an American Football player who plays for the Green Bay Packers in the NFL. He was picked in the second round of 2014 NFL Draft by the Packers and has been with them ever since. He plays as a wide receiver and wears jersey no. 17.

Adams was born as Davante Lavell Adams on December 24, 1992, to parents Douglas Adams and Pamela Brown. He has three siblings, one brother, and two sisters. He attended Palo Alto High School and played for the school team and led them to the CIF state championship.

The wide receiver also played basketball during his high school days and was considered a two-star recruit, but he later opts for football. He got recruited by California University, Hawaii, San Diego State, and California State University. He chooses California State University, where he would play his college football for Fresno State Bulldogs football team.

Who is Davante Adams' wife?

Adams is happily married to his College sweetheart Devanne J. Villarreal. The couple met each other when both of them attended Fresno State. The couple is together since then and even posts many pictures of his partner now and then. At Adams' 2014 NFL Draft, his beau Villarreal was also present.

Adams surprised his long-time girlfriend on July 6, 2017, when the couple was in the Bahamas for a photoshoot. He proposed to her on the beach, and she said YES!!.

The couple eventually tied the knot on June 2, 2018, giving the wedding invitation to the closed ones, including their family. His fellow players like Bryan Bulaga and Aaron Rodgers also attended the ceremony.

Both the bride and groom were looking stunning in a white strapless gown and black tuxedo. Their marriage venue was decorated with bright lights and several beautiful flowers.
