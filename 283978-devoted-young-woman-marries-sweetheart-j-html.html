Devoted Young Woman Marries Sweetheart Just One Day before He Passes Away from Cancer

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  537 words  |  Tandra Barner

Maia Falwasser proved that sickness could not stop her from marrying her man, as she tied the knot with Navar Herbert one day before he passed away from a brain tumor. Footage capturing the event left many in tears.

On June 24, 2019, a woman identified as Maia Falwasser married the love of her life just one day before he died of a brain tumor. The lady shared a short clip of the poignant ceremony in which she married the father of her son and best friend, Navar Herbert.

Guests present performed an emotional haka as family and friends shed tears in a romantic garden on Australia's Gold Coast. Herbert passed away on the night of June 25, 2019, after a long battle with a cancerous sarcoma tumor.

A picture of Navar Herbert and Maia Falwasser with their family on their wedding day | Photo: twitter.com/Metropoles

The Gold Coast Rugby player was allegedly diagnosed with the ailment several months before his demise, and as months passed, his condition rapidly deteriorated.

At a point, it was revealed that there was no cure for Herbert's illness, and his family was bent on giving him the best care until his last day. One week before he died, the man asked that his family take him to a mall.

There, he bought an engagement ring and proposed to Falwasser, with whom he shared an 11-month-old son. The beautiful lady agreed, and the couple was set to get married.

A few hours before they tied the knot, Herbert told his mother, Vanessa, "Today is the day I die." Immediately, the mother rebuked her son, assuring him that his brother was coming and he would get married at 4 pm.

The sick man eventually passed away 30 hours and 34 minutes after the ceremony.

Herbert hinged on his mother's words of positivity, and at 4 pm that day, the couple finally walked down the aisle in front of Herbert's brother, Kohan. The sick man eventually passed away 30 hours and 34 minutes after the ceremony. After his death, Falwasser shared the footage of the memorable day on a GoFundMe page.

A picture of Maia Falwasser getting ready for her wedding | Photo: Youtube/deeteevids

The page was set up to raise money to transport Herbert's body back to his hometown, Kotahitanga Marae, in New Zealand. In the video, Herbert's groomsmen were seen helping him dress up for the big day before wheeling him outside to await his bride. As she appeared at his side, he held her hands and said:

"Hello my wife, hello my baby. I love you."

The couple and guests fought tears as Herbert and Falwasser exchanged rings and swore to be partners for life. In a moving message attached to the video, the wife said she felt showered with love by her friends and family.

Navar Herbert and Maia Falwasser on their wedding day | Photo: Youtube/deeteevids

Falwasser noted that it was difficult, but she found strength and refuge in everyone's support. Three days after Herbert's demise, his Rugby club, the Nerang Bulls, hosted a celebration of his life. Although the young man is late, his memories will always be celebrated by his wife, rugby team, immediate family, and friends.
