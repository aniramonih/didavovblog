Given Brittney Griner's status as a renowned basketball sensation in the Women's National Basketball Association (WNBA), fans have been curious whether her siblings share her passion for sports.

WNBA star Brittney Griner feels incredibly fortunate to have a strong and supportive family, especially during times of crisis. Her siblings have been unwavering in their support, standing by her side through thick and thin.

While fans are well-acquainted with the athlete's impressive feats on the court and the recent news about her past detainment in another country, their curiosity extends to her siblings' lives and whereabouts.

Brittney Griner of the Phoenix Mercury during the game against the Indiana Fever on September 6, 2021, in Indianapolis, Indiana. | Source: Getty Images

Brittney Griner Has Three Siblings

Throughout her professional basketball career and trying times, Brittney received constant support from her three siblings: her brother DeCarlo Griner and her two sisters, Pleshette "Pier" Griner and SheKera Griner.

While little information is available regarding Brittney's siblings, social media posts from them and her 2014 memoir "In My Skin: My Life On and Off the Basketball Court" have shed light on their close connection.

Brittney Griner's Siblings Are Not into Sports but Are Proud Supporters of Her Life & Career

DeCarlo is Brittney's oldest sibling. He is 17 years older than the basketball star. His current profession remains unknown, but his Facebook profile states that he graduated from Nimitz Senior High School in 1990.

DeCarlo is a single man and a proud father of one child, Sydney Alyse Griner, whom he affectionately calls his "princess." Just as he shows his love for his daughter, he doesn't hesitate to express affection for his sister, Brittney.

In 2022, he sent his birthday wishes to Brittney through Facebook, even though he could not see or hear her voice. His message came when his sister faced detainment in Russia, making it one of the "most difficult days" of his life.

In his post, DeCarlo expressed his emotions, declaring his love and support for his little sister, eagerly looking forward to the day she would return. He also fervently prayed for her swift return home.

He is also close to his two sisters, Pleshette and SheKera. In 2021, he fondly wrote on Facebook that Pleshette has always been there for him when he needs "a joke & a bottle," while he noted SheKera as his "best friend."

Pleshette, also known as Pier, is five years older than Brittney. She frequently posts content about her famous sibling on her Facebook page, highlighting their close bond. However, this wasn't always the case.

Brittney revealed that the two could not be more different. Pier was a total girly girl, while Brittney was always known for having a "rough and tumble" personality.

In her memoir, Brittney also spoke about her relationship with Pleshette.

Meanwhile, SheKera, ten years older than Brittney, is the most private among the four siblings. Despite her privacy, she shared two noteworthy posts about her sister last year.

First, SheKera shared a screenshot from one of Brittney's coach's social media accounts. The coach had plans to visit the White House and address the athlete's wrongful detention in Russia.

For the second time, SheKera released a public statement on behalf of the Griner family, passionately urging President Joe Biden's administration to take every possible measure to bring her sister back home.

The Siblings' Real Relationship with Brittney Griner

Over the years, DeCarlo, Pleshette, and SheKera's strong connection with their renowned sibling, Brittney Griner, has seemingly grown stronger. Nothing could deter them from wholeheartedly supporting their little sister, no matter the circumstances. The athlete shared in her 2014 memoir:

"They have always been great to me and we consider each other full blood..."

In her memoir, Brittney also spoke about her relationship with Pleshette, emphasizing that they have grown much closer than they were in the past.

Brittney's remarkable career and captivating perspectives on life, as documented in her memoir, have made her one of the most followed basketball stars on both social media and in real life.
