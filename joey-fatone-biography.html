Who is Joey Fatone?

Joey Fatone is an American singer, dancer, and TV star. He is also an actor who has appeared in both TV and movies.

Joey Fatone: Age, Siblings, Parents, Ethnicity

Joey Fatone was born Joseph Anthony Fatone Jr. on Jan 28, 1977, in Brooklyn, New York City, New York, US.

His mother’s and father’s names are Phyllis Fatone and Joseph Anthony Fatone, Sr. respectively.

Mrs. Phyllis is a homemaker and Mr. Joseph is an actor plus musician.

He has an older sister named Janine and an older brother named Steven. He belongs to the ethnicity of Italian.

Education

Regarding his academic information, Joey Fatone joined Joseph B. Cavallaro J.H.S. in Brooklyn, New York City.

After this, he enrolled and graduated from Dr. Phillips High School, Orlando.

Joey Fatone: Professional Life, Career

Joey Fatone is a popular face in the entertainment industry.

He has also appeared in a dance reality show, Dancing with the Stars. He first participated in 2004 for season 4 but ended as a runner-up.

Then again in 2012 for season 15. This time also he was paired with Kym Johnson.

But unfortunately, he got evicted in the second week. After this, Joey performed in season 27 in 2018.

As a Singer

Joey Fatone was a member of the popular band NSYNC. It was formed in 1995 and was dissolved in 2002.

The other members of this boyband were Justin Timberlake, Chris Kirkpatrick, JC Chasez, and Lance Bass.

This group released its first album titled NYSNC in 1998. Then in 2000 and 2001, their second and third albums No Strings Attached and Celebrity came out respectively.

As an Actor

Joey Fatone was featured in movies like Longshot (2000) as Chef, My Big Fat Greek Wedding (2002) as Angelo, The Cooler (2003) as Johnny, Dead 7 (2016) as Whiskey Joe, Izzie’s Way Home (2013) as Carl, and more.

Moreover, Joey’s TV roles and hosting include Hannah Montana (2008), Impractical Jokes (2014, 2016, 2017), Family Feud, The Masked Singer (2019), Common Knowledge (2019), and more.
