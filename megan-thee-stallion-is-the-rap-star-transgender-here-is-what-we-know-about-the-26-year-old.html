Career 

The singer’s first single, “Like A Stallion,” was released in April 2016 and helped launch her career. She followed up with two more mixtapes, “Rich Ratchet” and “Megan Mix,” all of which were non-commercial.

“Make It Hot” was the title of her first commercially released mixtape. She began working with the 1501 Certified Entertainment label in 2018, becoming the label’s first female rapper.

In June of the same year, she released her EP “Tina Snow,” which was titled after the singers alter ego. Her second mixtape, “Fever,” was one of the year’s most successful releases.

She’s released several singles off the album since then, collaborating with artists such as Chance The Rapper, Nicki Minaj, and Ty Dolla Sign. A viral meme was developed as a result of her song “Hot Girl Summer,” and these phrases became one of the most popular Instagram captions. 

The rapper secured a deal with Roc Nation in September 2019, and two months later, she was named to the 2019 TIME 100 Next list.

She followed up with a couple more singles and her EP “Suga” in 2020. Megan Thee Stallion’s song “Savage,” from this EP, went viral on TikTok and YouTube. She also made an appearance in Cardi B’s single “WAP” and in the music video for the song.

The Megan Thee Stallion tour was meant to take place in 2020, however owing to the global pandemic, practically all of the dates were postponed.
