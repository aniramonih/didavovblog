Early Life

Snooki was born on 23rd November 1987 as Nicole Elizabeth Polizzi in Santiago, Chile. She was adopted by an Italian American couple when she was six months old. Her father Andy Polizzi is an auto salvage supervisor and a volunteer firefighter. Her mother, Helen Polizzi, is an office manager.

Snooki went to Marlboro High School in New York. This is where she was given the sobriquet “Snooki” by her friends. Later on, she went to community college to study for a career as a veterinary technician.

Personal Life

Snooki is married to businessman Jionni LaValle. It is reported that Jionni LaValle has his own T-shirt line and ATM business. They have two children together.

Currently, Snooki is living with her family in Florham Park, New Jersey, and runs her own clothing shop called the Snooki Shop.

Career

Snooki started her career in TV through an MTV show Is she really going out with him. Later on, she was cast for the reality TV series The Jersey Shore Show.

Snooki became the most popular member of the Jersey show cast because of her on-screen antics. Once the show ended, Snooki paired with her costar Jennifer Farley to star in another show called Snooki and JWoww. The show ran for four seasons and ended in 2015.

Snooki has also appeared with her husband on the show Nicole & Jionni’s Shore Flip.

Other than TV shows, Snooki has also appeared on various award shows like MTV Music Awards as a presenter. She has also appeared as a contestant on the Dancing with the Stars show. She finished in the 8th position in the show.

Snooki has also appeared as a wrestler in the WWE show.

She has authored three books, A shore thing, Confessions of a Guidette, and Gorilla Beach.

Snooki is currently shooting for a revival show of Jersey Shore titled The Family Vacation. The reunion show will have its old cast together again.
