Eddie Murphy's Son Miles and Grandbaby Pose Together in a Heart-Warming Photo

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  538 words  |  Tandra Barner

Comedy actor Eddie Murphy's son, Miles Mitchell, bears a striking resemblance with his famous father as he poses with his cute daughter, Evie, in a recent heart-melting photo.

Despite having a large family, veteran actor Eddie Murphy only recently became a grandfather. In 2019, the standup comedian welcomed his granddaughter, Evie, from his son, Miles Mitchell, whom he shares with his ex-wife Nicole Murphy.

The proud grandmother has been doting on the little angel ever since. She recently took to her Instagram page to share an adorable picture of her son and her granddaughter.

Nicole Murphy at the 28th Annual Talk Of The Town Gala in 2017. | Photo: Getty Images

In the picture, Miles, who is the spitting image of his father, can be seen in a beautiful outdoor environment, adorning a yellow long-sleeved shirt and blue shorts while he cradles his 11-month-old daughter, who is dressed in a pink outfit.

Evie completely steals the show with her adorable smile, and many fans couldn't hold back their admiration for the beautiful little princess. Nicole expressed her joy in the caption. She wrote:

"These two right here. My loves #mybabies #myles #evie #son #grandbaby #myloves #sohappy #smile"

Miles welcomed his daughter, Evie Isla Murphy, on July 2, 2019, with his girlfriend, Carly Olivia. The latest addition to the Murphy family was well received by family members, especially the first-time grandfather.

Last year, Murphy, 58, who is back in the limelight after the success of his latest movie "Dolemite Is My Name," was spotted in a cute picture with his granddaughter during the holidays in 2019.

Eddie Murphy may have a large family, but this blended family is tightly-knit.

In the picture which was uploaded by Carly Olivia, the "Coming to America" star is sporting a blue and red long-sleeved shirt and is holding on to the beautiful Evie, who had on a white dress and pink fur.

Meanwhile, the iconic actor sensationally welcomed his 10th child, son Max Charles Murphy, with his fiancee Paige Butcher barely six months before his granddaughter was born. During a 2019 appearance on Live! Murphy said:

"I have my first grandchild. So I think she's five months — a five-month-old grandchild and a 10-month-old son. Guess that makes me a heck of a fella..."

Murphy also shares a daughter, Izzy Oona, 4, with his fiancee Butcher. The comedian has five other children, daughters Bella Zahra, 18, Zola Ivy, 20, Shayne Audra, 25, and Bria, 30, and son Miles Mitchell, 27 — with his ex-wife Nicole.

The iconic actor has three other children from previous relationships. Murphy's eldest son Eric, 30, with Paulette McNeely. He also shares a son, Christian, 29, with Tamara Hood and a daughter Angel Iris, 12, with former "Spice Girl," Mel B.

Back in December, the talented actor got his family together for a rare family photo. The picture featured the actor, all ten of his children, including his newborn son. Also in the picture was his fiancee, Butcher, and her mom and finally Murphy's mother, Lillian.

Eddie Murphy may have a large family, but this blended family is tightly-knit, and the proud father does his best to be there for all his kids.
